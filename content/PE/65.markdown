<div> $ {\displaystyle
\begin{eqnarray}
p_n=\left\{ \begin{array}{ll}  
1 & (n=0)\\  
a_0 & (n=1)\\  
a_{n-1} p_{n-1} + p_{n-2} & (n\geq2) \\  
\end{array} \right.  
\end{eqnarray}
} $</div>
